My Projects

Solar Mobile Charger

Designed and built a portable solar mobile charger with a focus on durability, modularity, and solar efficiency. Evolved from a box to a prismatic structure for improved stability and performance. Used aluminum bracing, MDF panels, and zip-tie assembly to enable low-tech fabrication and easy repair. Achieved lightweight design under 10 kg with optimized solar orientation and thermal resistance.

Wireless Backlit Keyboard

Designed a custom wireless backlit keyboard, focusing on affordability, ergonomics, and modularity. Created a detailed CAD model optimized for ease of assembly and repair using low-cost materials. Integrated design features for wireless use and LED backlighting without increasing complexity. Served as a concept exploration in combining electronics layout with mechanical design principles.

Mini Drone

Designed a compact, lightweight mini quadcopter with a fully 3D-printable frame. Focused on structural simplicity, modularity, and ease of assembly for quick prototyping. Optimized for small-scale flight testing with minimal material usage and efficient layout. Demonstrates skills in 3D modeling, aerodynamics, and integrating mechanical design with electronics.

FSAE Rear Wing

Designed a rear wing for the Formula Student car with a focus on aerodynamic efficiency and structural integrity. Optimized airfoil profiles and endplate geometry to maximize downforce while minimizing drag. Integrated mounting points into the chassis design to ensure stability under high-speed conditions. Balanced performance, manufacturability, and weight to align with overall vehicle dynamics goals.
